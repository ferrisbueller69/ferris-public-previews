About The District

The corner of Campbell where flavor meets fun.

We opened The District to bring the neighborhood something real — a place where the burgers are built by hand, the cocktails are poured by people who actually care, and the game is always on.

Whether you're rolling in for happy hour, crashing our patio on a Saturday night, or sneaking away for a Tuesday date, we'll treat you like a regular from the first pour.
