The Boutique

Curated like a closet.
Run like a community.

Boutique 816 opened in 2012 as the more laid-back sister to Collette — same eye, different tempo. Easier silhouettes, beachier prints, festival staples that don't try too hard.

Owner Amy DeVries Jesionowski opened the original Collette in 2011, and four doors east, Boutique 816 followed a year later. The shops are part of the rhythm of University Boulevard — first-time freshmen, fifth-year seniors, alumni back for game day, anyone hunting that one perfect coverup before a Catalina trip.

Every piece is hand-selected, the sizes are real, and the fitting room playlist is chronically debated by everyone on staff.
